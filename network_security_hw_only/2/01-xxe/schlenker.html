NetSec Registration

This is the beta test of our new NetSec registration system.

To register, please download our registration template, fill it out, and POST it to the endpoint https://schlenkerla.xxe.netsec.seclab-bonn.de/register/.

You can do this with curl like so (more or less):

$ curl -X POST -d @reg-template.xml https://schlenkerla.xxe.netsec.seclab-bonn.de/register/

Have fun!
